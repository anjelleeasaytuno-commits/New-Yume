# New Yume

A psychological visual novel made with Ren'Py

## About

New Yume is a psychological visual novel about a girl named Yume who wakes up inside a mysterious abandoned church. There, she meets two guiding figures: Yoru, who offers comfort and escape through an idealized dream world, and Satoshi, who represents painful truth and reality. As Yume explores the church, she is forced to choose between staying in a comforting illusion or facing a harsh awakening. The game explores themes of escapism, trauma, and the thin line between fantasy and reality.

## How to Play
 Click to progress through the story and dialogue. game has two endings, choices throughout the game determines the players ending.
